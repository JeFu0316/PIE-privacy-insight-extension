    Terms & clarity

    How scores work

    Sensitivity and tracking are two independent readings — not one blended score. Real personal data is never confused with ordinary tracker IDs.

    Cookies on a site

    Overview messages such as “no personal data in cookies” or “this site loads known cross-site trackers” describe what Toolingo detected on the page you are viewing. They are explanations, not a single risk grade.

    On-device by design

    Cookie analysis, tracker classification, network logging, and cleaning run on your device. Toolingo does not send your cookies, browsing history, or request contents to a developer server.

    Network tab

    Observed on your device — metadata only. Toolingo never records request bodies or page contents, and does not upload the Network list.

    Settings & digest

    Settings may sync across Chrome when you are signed in. The weekly digest stores only aggregate tracker counts and cleaned-cookie totals locally — not the first-party sites you visit.
